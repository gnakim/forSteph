geena got really
mad at me because this
letter
was so
late but i couldn’t bring myself to start writing
words on a page because nothing felt
good enough,
sufficiently genuine or
true to how much i
love you.

it’s a different kind of love you and
i talk about love all the time and how much we
love it. are intoxicated by boys and kissing and
alcohol and i question all of these loves but never
ours

because it’s a different kind of love we met in a
different kind of way: in the
woods away from the
noise of fake friendships and distractions
we had no choice but to share our life stories, and i figured i’d rather share mine with
you than with the wind so i

haven’t looked back since we were among the
trees

you have been my best friend and my
worst friend worst when you
care about me too much when you
tell me i’m wrong, or paranoid, or
right.

because you are usually right, calm, and or wrong in those moments
respectively
i am so grateful for those moments
i am so thankful i get to call you my friend and
as we both know
im not religious
but i do consider you a
